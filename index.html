Board Governance Development

Sometimes ensuring that your organization succeeds seems more difficult than you expected, particularly when the members of your board have lots of skills but not the understanding of how to apply them in a board context. When boards, CEOs, and leadership teams thrive, entire organizations succeed with them. But the effectiveness of your leadership team may be endangered by such issues as personal differences, lack of strategic alignment, or failures of structure and governance. Larry advises Boards on how to optimize their overall effectiveness, committee effectiveness, and individual director effectiveness. He assists CEOs to optimize leadership effectiveness, impact, and legacy.

He can help you achieve alignment on strategic direction and risk, secure the right people for the right executive roles, and build the leadership capabilities necessary to deliver superior business performance.

Improving the performance and impact of the board.

Research indicates that there are three key elements that influence board effectiveness:

  • Composition – does the board have the right mix of members?
  • Focus – do the members of the board have a shared focus on the organization’s strategy and capabilities?
  • Team dynamics – could board members be working together more effectively?

Focus on these three elements in concert while helping to build an effective governance model. Larry is able to offer solutions in a range of areas, including board composition, strategic alignment, team effectiveness, and succession.

Board Best Practices

When boards, CEOs and leadership teams thrive, entire organizations succeed with them. Governance and compliance remain significant issues that Boards must address, but are only part of the story. Forward-looking boards should move ‘beyond compliance’ and focus on behavioral and relationship issues that shape board dynamics and drive board and organizational performance. Through workshops, Board Policy Manual development and one-on-one coaching, we help boards provide structure and shape relationships, culture and the team dynamics that underpin success.

Birkman Method Assessment

The Birkman Method® is a confidential assessment which measures strengths, motivations, interests, and stress behaviors that will impact job performance and relationships. Our certified Birkman consultant will partner with you to unpack the findings, identify objective and reliable insights, and help you work with those insights to build your next outstanding executive team.

About

Larry Nelson, BA FCA, is a graduate of Simon Fraser University and became a Chartered Accountant. He worked in industry and public practice for many years before eventually transitioning into the not–for–profit sector.

Active in local politics, he was elected to the Coquitlam school board in the 1980s and served as a trustee for three terms. A lifelong interest in affordable housing led to his work with several not–for–profit organizations — the BC Non–Profit Housing Association, Monarch Place transitional housing for women with children fleeing domestic abuse, and the New Hope Community Services Society, which provided refugee housing.

For several years Larry served as board chair for the Canadian Council of Christian Charities and currently serves on the leadership advisory council for Trinity Western University's School of Business. Larry worked with the Baptist Housing Ministries Society for 13 years as its chief executive officer and retired from that position in 2007. During his association with the organization Larry assisted in the creation of retiremet housing for more than 1,800 seniors in comunities throughout BC. Today he continues to serve on a number of boards including as chair of the board of governors of Northwest Baptist Seminary.

Larry is currently a senior associate with the executive search firm Hutchinson Group, Inc., which focuses on the not-for-profit sector and has offices in Vancouver, Toronto and Ottawa. He also assists boards in conducting organization, policy and program reviews and board governance consulting in the areas of strategic planning, best board practices and succession planning.
